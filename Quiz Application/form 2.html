National Open University of Nigeria - CIT 206 Quiz Examination

Quiz Instructions

Welcome to the CIT206 Quiz!

Please follow the instructions below to take the quiz:

  1. Log in using your username and password above.
  2. Click on the "Login" button.
  3. You will be redirected to the quiz page.
  4. Read each question carefully.
  5. Select the correct answer by clicking on the option.
  6. Once you have answered all the questions, click on the "Submit" button.
  7. Your quiz results will be displayed.

Question Instructions

Please keep the following instructions in mind while answering the questions:

  • Read each question carefully before selecting an answer.
  • Select the best possible answer based on your understanding.
  • If the question has multiple correct answers, select all that apply.
  • If you are unsure about an answer, you can skip the question and come back to it later.
  • Review your answers before submitting the quiz.
  • Click on the "Submit" button to submit your quiz.
